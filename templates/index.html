Tesla

The purpose of this project is to analyze the performance of the EV car company, Tesla. By using Machine Learning and historical data, we can see the current performance of Tesla and see how well will they perform in the future. Tesla Inc is an electric vehicle and energy company and it's based in Palo Alto, California. The company was found in 2003 as a tribute to a famous inventor, Nikola Tesla and their current CEO is Elon Musk, who has served as that positon since 2008. Their primary product is the electronic vehicles and its current lineup is the following:

  • Model S
  • Model 3
  • Model X
  • Model Y
  • Roadster
  • Tesla Semi
  • Cybertruck
