Summary Analysis

Tesla is the leader in the EV market, thier models are among the top 6 selling cars. The model3, Tesla's most affordable model, has sold close to 300,000 units as of 2019. The worst perfomring model(modelX) is also the most expensive one and an SUV style vehicle. By continuing to advance the technology needed to make eclectic cars and lowering the cost of their entry level model3 Tesla should be able to further grow their market share and begin to gain more of a foothold in the overall car market.
